## Protein anchors by cuisine

- **Mediterranean** — chicken, fish (salmon, sardines, branzino), legumes (chickpeas, lentils), eggs, Greek yogurt, feta
- **Persian** — chicken, lamb, beef, fish, beans (red kidney, white), eggs, yogurt
- **Indian** — chicken, lamb, lentils (dal), chickpeas, paneer, eggs, yogurt, fish (coastal)
- **East Asian** — chicken, pork, beef, tofu, tempeh, eggs, fish (salmon, mackerel)
- **Italian** — chicken, fish, beans (cannellini, borlotti), prosciutto/pancetta sparingly, eggs

## Prep convergence patterns

These are the workflows that make a meal plan actually work in real life.

### Sunday cook day
- 1 large protein (whole roast chicken, batch of chicken thighs, or pork loin) → covers Sun dinner + Mon lunch + Tue dinner
- 1 large grain (brown rice, quinoa, or farro) → covers 3–4 lunches/dinners
- Roasted veg trays (sweet potato, broccoli, peppers) → topping for bowls all week
- Hard-boiled eggs (8–12) → snacks + breakfast additions

### Tuesday top-up
- Cook a different protein mid-week (fish or tofu) so the second half of the week doesn't feel repetitive
- Wash and chop fresh produce (cucumber, peppers, herbs) for the rest of the week's salads

### Theme nights
- Predictable themes lower decision fatigue: Sun roast, Mon leftovers, Tue grain bowl, Wed fish, Thu stir-fry, Fri pizza/wraps, Sat free choice

## Couples' portions

When planning for two with different targets (e.g., 1700 kcal + 2200 kcal):
- Cook the same dish, scale portions
- Add a starch side (rice, bread) for the higher-target person to top up calories
- Keep protein portions roughly proportional to each person's protein target

## Leftover-management notation

In the plan, mark meals with:
- `prep` — cooked in a dedicated session, eaten cold or reheated later
- `→Mon` — leftover that becomes Monday's meal
- `core` — uses the week's core protein/grain/veg
- `quick` — under 30 min start to plate

## Common ingredient swaps

When a user is missing an ingredient or wants variety:
| Original | Swap |
|---|---|
| Quinoa | Farro, brown rice, bulgur |
| Chicken breast | Chicken thigh, turkey breast, tofu |
| Salmon | Mackerel, trout, canned salmon |
| Greek yogurt | Skyr, cottage cheese (similar protein) |
| Almond butter | Peanut butter, sunflower seed butter |
| Olive oil | Avocado oil, walnut oil |
| Sweet potato | Butternut squash, regular potato |
| Spinach | Kale, arugula, Swiss chard |

Document the swap column so the user can adapt without asking again.
