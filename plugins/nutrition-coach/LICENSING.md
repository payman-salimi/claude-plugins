## Health disclaimer

This plugin provides general nutrition guidance based on established frameworks, including the U.S. Dietary Guidelines for Americans 2025-2030 and standard practice in registered-dietitian and certified-nutritionist curricula. It is not a substitute for personalized medical or dietary advice from a licensed healthcare provider. Users with medical conditions, pregnancy, eating disorders, or specific clinical needs should consult a registered dietitian or physician before following any meal plan generated by this plugin.
